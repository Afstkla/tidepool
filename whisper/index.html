your word is drifting
A poem no one writes, built from words strangers leave behind.
how it works
Each visitor can leave a single word. It joins the others, drifting through space, forming accidental phrases with its neighbors. Every word fades after three days. The poem is never the same twice. A small set of connective words — the, and, but, in — are always present, providing structure for the rest.
interact
Type a word and press enter to add it to the drift. Hover near words to gently draw them toward you.
